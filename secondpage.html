Tea

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Tea leaves or tea bags
  • Milk (optional)
  • Sugar or sweetener (optional)

Here's a brief overview of ingredient quantities for making tea:

  • Water: Typically, you'll need about 1 cup (240 ml) per serving of tea.
  • Tea leaves or tea bags: Use 1 teaspoon of loose tea leaves or 1 tea bag per cup of water.
  • Milk: Optional, add according to preference, usually about 1/4 to 1/2 cup per serving.
  • Sugar or sweetener: Optional, add according to taste, typically 1-2 teaspoons per cup.
  • Adjust quantities based on personal preferences for strength and sweetness.

Recipe:

  • Boil water in a pot or kettle.
  • Add tea leaves or tea bags to the boiling water.
  • Let the tea steep for 3-5 minutes, depending on desired strength.
  • If using milk, add it to the tea and let it simmer for a minute.
  • Add sugar or sweetener to taste.
  • Strain the tea into cups and serve hot

    • Enjoy your cup of tea!
