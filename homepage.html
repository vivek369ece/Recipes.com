Biryani

Here is the recipe to make the Biryani in home easily!!!

Ingredients for Biryani

  • Basmati rice
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Fresh cilantro and mint leaves
  • Ghee or oil
  • Nuts and raisins (optional)
  • Spices

Details of Indgredients

Basmati rice: Typically, for every cup of rice, you'll need about 1.5 to 2 cups of water for cooking.
Chicken, lamb, or vegetables: Use around 500 grams to 1 kilogram, depending on how many servings you're making.
Onions: About 3 to 4 medium-sized onions, thinly sliced.
Tomatoes: 2 to 3 medium-sized tomatoes, chopped.
Ginger-garlic paste: 2 tablespoons.
Yogurt: 1 cup.
Spices: Use about 1 teaspoon each of cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder, along with 2 teaspoons of garam masala. You can adjust to taste.
Fresh cilantro and mint leaves: A handful of each, chopped.
Ghee or oil: 3 to 4 tablespoons.
Saffron strands: A pinch, soaked in 2 tablespoons of warm milk (optional).
Fried onions: Optional, about 1/2 cup.
Nuts and raisins: Optional, about 1/4 cup each.
Adjust quantities according to your taste preferences and the number of servings you're preparing.

Process

  1. Soak rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. Marinate meat or vegetables with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices.
  3. In a separate pan, sauté onions until golden brown. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Layer half of the rice in a pot, then add the marinated meat/vegetables and half of the sautéed onions and tomatoes.
  5. Repeat the layers with the remaining rice, onions, and tomatoes.
  6. Add saffron soaked in warm milk, fried onions, nuts, and raisins on top.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is cooked and the flavors meld together.

    8. Enjoy your flavorful Biryani!
